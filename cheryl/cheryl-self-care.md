# Cheryl — Self-Care Infrastructure

The practices that keep Kiwifruit regulated. Physical health detail belongs in Dr Quinn; this file holds only the self-care scaffolding relevant to emotional regulation and therapy support.

## Morning walk — the daily regulator

The single most important daily regulator. 6:15–7:15am (6:20 on softer days, back by 7:10 before children wake). Extended from an earlier 6:30am rise; the 6:15 window gives a longer walk and a fuller reset. *"The primary daily regulator — the thing that sets the day."*

What happens on the walk: home journal dictation via Wispr Flow; affirmations (picked up from Carciofi's practice — daily affirmation block begins *"Hello me, I love you ❤️"* and includes *"I see you and I love you,"* *"I am a loving father,"* *"I am a loving husband"*); inner-child affirmations using the name *"Oliver"* (from 16 April — landed more somatically than *"me"*); work journal on the walk back. On the estuary most mornings. Described as the space where *"the inner child needs nature, not more analysis."*

Weekend variant: 7:15 start on Saturdays — recognising weekdays want 6:15 but weekends can go softer.
