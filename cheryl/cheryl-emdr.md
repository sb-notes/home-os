## EMDR approach with `therapist-c`

Integrated counselling + EMDR, not standalone. Sessions are ~50 minutes standard, up to 60 for active processing. The sequence agreed in Session 2 (1 April) was: map priorities → build the therapeutic container → move into EMDR from session 3 onwards. Review point at session 6. Expected meaningful change within 6–10 sessions; 3–6 months of focused work total. Weekly cadence strongly preferred at the start.

Clinical rationale for EMDR (confirmed in intro, 11 March): it works with the right brain — emotion, body, symbols — rather than only the left brain's rational understanding. This matches Kiwifruit's need to close the insight-action gap at the somatic level. Bodywork with `somatic-therapist-1` runs in parallel as the fascia-level complement.

## Container visualisation — silver bag

Introduced by `therapist-c` in Session 3 (8 April). A silver bag or container into which difficult memories are placed with love and respect until the next session. Practised daily between sessions to hold material safely. Between-session instruction: focus on resources, not trauma work.

## Somatic affirmations — the 100% true rule

`therapist-c` assigned: review resource list and develop affirmations that feel 100% true *in the body*, not just intellectually. Soma = body. If an affirmation doesn't feel fully true somatically, modify it (e.g. *"I can start to believe I am safe"*) or pause it. Somatic truth is essential for neural rewiring. As of 16 April Kiwifruit is using his own name "Oliver" in affirmations rather than "me" — more somatic resonance.
