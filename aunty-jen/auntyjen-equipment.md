## Pressure Cooker / Air Fryer Combo

**Instant Pot Pro Crisp 8L**
- 11-in-1 multi-cooker: pressure cook, air fry, slow cook, steam, sauté, bake, roast, dehydrate, sous vide, warm, grill
- 8-litre capacity
- Silicone inserts in use (for easy cleaning and to protect the liner)
- Key distinction: has both the pressure lid and the air fryer lid — can pressure cook then switch to air crisp in one pot
- Common uses: braises, stews, beans, rice, crispy finishing on roasts

## Air Fryer

**Ninja Combi (Ninja Combi Multi-Cooker, Oven & Air Fryer)**
- Large capacity combi unit — functions as air fryer, oven, and multi-cooker
- Sits on the counter as a standalone unit
- Good for batch cooking, baking, and air frying larger quantities than the Instant Pot lid allows

## Notes for Aunty Jen

- Always clarify which appliance is being used in recipe steps — Ollie has both the Instant Pot air fryer lid and the Ninja Combi; they behave differently (basket size, airflow, temp calibration)
- Ceramic hob on the Cookmaster heats more slowly than induction — factor into timing
- Silicone inserts in the Instant Pot should be specified when they affect liquid levels or cooking behaviour
