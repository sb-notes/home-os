## SHULA'S STANDING ADVICE — KEY PRINCIPLES
- Do not choose cheapest managing agent — you get what you pay for
- Large decisions wait for the 6 directors' meetings per year — avoid reactive decision-making
- Reach consensus rather than vote on decisions
- AGM: re-election of directors, approval of accounts, close — then informal round table (no minutes)
- Budget meeting one month before AGM
- All leaseholders can view invoices at any time via Xero
- RTM company is dormant — get an accountant to file dormant company accounts annually (cost from service charge)
- Block accounts are income and expenditure only — not profit and loss — not filed
- Service charge arrears chased by managing agent, not directors directly
- Federation of Private Residents' Association (FPRA) membership recommended — good for legal advice and buildings insurance
- Get a proper lease interpretation at the outset
- Section 20 consultation required for any major works exceeding £250 per leaseholder
