### Bedding & Mattresses

#### Master Bedroom Mattress

- **Model:** Latex Sense Sensation Hybrid King
- **Purchased:** Feb 2022 (~4 years old)
- **Cover:** Tencel outer cover, zip-access. **Never machine washed.**
- **Weight:** 56kg — manoeuvring to remove/refit cover is a significant task; two-person job

**Stain treatment (Apr 2026):**
- Protein stain (palm-sized, penetrated through sheet to cover)
- Treated in-situ: diluted white wine vinegar (50/50 with water), dabbed with e-cloth, dried with dehumidifier
- Outcome: effective — most staining removed
- Key principle: keep all moisture surface-level only — latex beneath must not get wet

**Recommended protector:** Woolroom Deluxe Washable Wool Mattress Protector (King) — ~£85–120. Breathable, waterproof, suits latex. Worth buying now before next incident.

**Cover wash:** Consider 30°C gentle cycle at next opportunity. Never been done.

#### Children's Mattresses

- **Model:** Little Green Sheep Wool Coconut Core and Latex sleeping pads (both twins)
- **Protection:** Bamboo waterproof mattress protectors already fitted — adequate for bed-wetting stage
- No further protectors needed at this time

#### Mattress Cleaning Protocol (reference)

| Stain type | Method |
|---|---|
| Protein (urine, blood) | Cold water + biological washing powder enzyme solution — dab, never rub, work inward from edges |
| Odour | Diluted white wine vinegar (50/50) — spray and dab |
| Deodorising | Bicarbonate of soda — leave 30–60 mins, vacuum off |
| Drying | Dehumidifier aimed at patch — never use heat |

**Toddler night-change hack:** Double-layer system — waterproof protector → sheet → second protector → second sheet. Enables 3-minute night change without full bed strip.
