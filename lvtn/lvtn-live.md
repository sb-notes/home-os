## Current Strategic Status

Violet has a working platform — LLM-powered perfume discovery with a growing data infrastructure, content engine, and affiliate monetisation. The product is real and technically impressive. The problem is traffic: no meaningful volume yet, no conversions.

**The pivot in progress:** away from "Last Minute Larry" (gifting) toward **24–34 year old women buying perfume for themselves**, driven by early Search Console data showing celebrity-driven impressions dominating.

**The brand crisis:** "Lavender Thorn" reads as traditional/granny to the target audience. A rebrand to an AI-forward name is recommended. Top candidate: **Perfuma** — coined, short, legally defensible, clean namespace. Reed and Slate currently in disagreement on direction.
