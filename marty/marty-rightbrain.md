## OneBrain project

Kiwifruit's first ownership item at Rightbrain. Ideal quick win: visible, dogfooding-aligned, finite, demonstrates product thinking.

**Two-hat model (finalised by Kiwifruit independently, validated in coaching):**
- **Customer Discovery Hat:** Every team 1:1 used as customer discovery interview after rapport-building. Use cases captured identically to external customer engagement, attributed to team member who raised them.
- **Builder/Product Hat:** Use cases ranked by value + effort; split Assembly (platform supports today) vs Build (requires dev). Assembly items sequenced first without waiting for eng.

**Assembly-first discipline:** Do not spec Build items until Assembly items are running and validated.

**Externally-demonstrable rule:** At least one OneBrain workflow must be packageable as a prospect proof point ("we built this for ourselves in 2 days").

**P0 target:** Transcript → insights pipeline. First automation: Gemini meeting transcripts → Notion pages automatically. Monitor two specific Google Drive folders; output to Notion. Demonstrated live on Day 1 — low effort, high visibility, directly ICP-relevant.
