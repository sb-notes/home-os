# Bob the Builder — House Reference

53 Victoria Road, Shoreham-by-Sea, BN43 5WR. Victorian mid-terrace, 3 storeys, ~1,297 sq ft / ~120 sq m.

---

## Orientation

The terrace runs east–west: **front (street side) faces east, rear (park side) faces west**. More precisely, the house is oriented east-northeast to west-southwest — the sun arc means it never sets directly into the middle of the rear garden in midsummer, sitting lower on the horizon year-round than a true due-west aspect would suggest.

- **Front** faces east (street). Living room bay window.
- **Rear** faces west (park/garden). Kitchen-diner, patio, garden beyond.

Practical implications: the rear kitchen-diner gets afternoon and evening sun — genuine west light, but lower and more raking than expected for the latitude. The front rooms get morning light. The loft west-facing mini dormer catches low evening sun directly.
